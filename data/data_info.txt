data/masks (Masks)
data/moisture (Substrate moisture)
data/observations/monitoring/destructive_cycleXX.csv
data/observations/monitoring/scales (Weight)
data/scans/cycleXX (LAI data - Folders are named Ciclo0N+1 instead of Cycle0N)
data/weather/monitoring/raw/li1400 (PAR)
data/weather/monitoring/raw/radio (Transdutors)
data/weather/monitoring/raw/sensorpi
